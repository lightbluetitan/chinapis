Licensing Information for ChinAPIs Package

This package, ChinAPIs, includes datasets sourced from various R packages. In order to maintain consistency and clarity, a naming convention has been applied where suffixes are added to the dataset names to indicate the type and format of the datasets. The original content of these datasets has not been altered in any way.
